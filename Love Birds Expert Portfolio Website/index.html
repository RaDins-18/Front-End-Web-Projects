About Me

Professional Love-Bird Breeder!

My name is Shafi Sheikh, and I am a lovebird breeder. I have been breeding lovebirds for the past 4 years and have developed a deep passion for these birds. I have a large aviary with several breeding pairs that I keep for breeding purposes. I also have a number of pet birds which I enjoy interacting with.

I have extensive knowledge in lovebird breeding, care, and nutrition. I am well-versed in the various species of lovebirds and understand the importance of genetic diversity and bloodlines. I strive to ensure that the birds I produce are healthy, strong, and beautiful.I am dedicated to creating a safe, healthy environment for my birds and ensure that they are provided with the best possible care.

My Services

Expert Lovebird Breeding

At Heartfelt Lovebirds, we specialize in breeding healthy, vibrant lovebirds with strong pair bonds. Our breeding pairs are carefully selected for their health, temperament, and genetic diversity, ensuring that each chick is robust and well-socialized. We provide a nurturing environment where lovebirds can thrive, complete with spacious aviaries, nutritious diets, and ample opportunities for natural behaviors.

Comprehensive Guidance

We offer comprehensive care for our lovebirds, including regular health check-ups, meticulous cleaning routines, and expert veterinary oversight. Our commitment to the well-being of our birds extends to their diet, with a balanced mix of seeds, pellets, fresh fruits, and vegetables, supplemented with essential nutrients. Additionally, we provide new owners with detailed care instructions and ongoing support to ensure their lovebirds lead healthy, happy lives.

Personalized Adoptions

At Heartfelt Lovebirds, we believe in creating lasting bonds between our birds and their new families. Each adoption is personalized to match the right bird with the right home, ensuring compatibility and mutual affection. We also foster a community of lovebird enthusiasts through workshops, newsletters, and social media, offering a platform for sharing experiences, advice, and the joys of lovebird companionship.
