About Me

Professional Love-Bird Breeder!

My name is Shafi Sheikh, and I am a lovebird breeder. I have been breeding lovebirds for the past 4 years and have developed a deep passion for these birds. I have a large aviary with several breeding pairs that I keep for breeding purposes. I also have a number of pet birds which I enjoy interacting with.

I have extensive knowledge in lovebird breeding, care, and nutrition. I am well-versed in the various species of lovebirds and understand the importance of genetic diversity and bloodlines. I strive to ensure that the birds I produce are healthy, strong, and beautiful.I am dedicated to creating a safe, healthy environment for my birds and ensure that they are provided with the best possible care.
